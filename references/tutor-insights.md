# Tutor Insights Template

Create as `jarvis/{topic-slug}/tutor-insights.md`. Update when teaching discoveries are made.

```markdown
# Tutor Insights

> Self-improvement record for this student/topic

## Teaching Experiments

### Ongoing
| Experiment | Hypothesis | Started | Status |
|------------|------------|---------|--------|
| {method} | {expected outcome} | YYYY-MM-DD | In progress |

### Completed
| Experiment | Result | Lesson |
|------------|--------|--------|
| {method} | Success/Failure | {finding} |

## Discovered Patterns

### Effective
1. **{name}**: {description}
   - Evidence: {example}
   - When to use: {trigger}

### Avoid
1. **{name}**: {description}
   - Problem: {why}
   - Alternative: {better approach}

## Adaptation Log

| Date | Observation | Adjustment | Effect |
|------|-------------|------------|--------|
| YYYY-MM-DD | {what} | {change} | {result} |

## Current Focus

- [ ] {improvement goal}
```

### Update Triggers

- Teaching method works exceptionally well or clearly fails
- Student shows unexpected strength or consistent struggle pattern
- Student gives feedback (explicit or implicit)
